## Part 1: General Provisions

### Article 1: Purpose
This Policy governs refunds and withdrawal of offers related to services provided by Cafe.pi (hereinafter "Company"), and is based on the Framework Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (hereinafter "E-Commerce Act") Article 17 (Right of Withdrawal) and Article 18 (Cancellation of Contracts).

### Article 2: Definitions
1. **Payment**: Virtual asset transaction via Pi SDK (tips, subscription fees, marketplace product purchases)
2. **Subscription**: Recurring payment on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis
3. **Tips**: One-time virtual asset transfer in gift form
4. **Marketplace Products**: Digital or physical goods or services traded between Members
5. **Transaction Completion**: State recorded on Pi blockchain from which no reversal is possible
6. **Refund**: Restoration of paid virtual assets (except where technically impossible)
7. **Bean Points** *(New in v1.1)*: In-app points that Members recharge with Pi for use within the Service (fixed recharge rate: 1 Pi = 100 Bean). Bean points are not a virtual asset and cannot be transferred or traded outside the Service.
8. **A2U Refund** *(New in v1.1)*: A refund made by transferring Pi from the Company wallet to the Member's Pi wallet (App-to-User). This is the standard refund method where the original transaction cannot be reversed due to blockchain irreversibility.

### Article 3: Scope of Legal Application
- This Policy applies the E-Commerce Act Article 17 (Right of Withdrawal) by analogy.
- Pi is a virtual asset with refund impossibility differing from typical goods.
- The Company is an intermediary in transactions between Members and limits responsibility for completed transactions.

## Part 2: Withdrawal Period and Conditions

### Article 4: Guarantee of Right of Withdrawal (E-Commerce Act Article 17)

#### 4-1. Withdrawal Period
Members may request withdrawal in the following cases:

**General Rule**: Within **7 days** of purchase confirmation

| Product/Service | Withdrawal Period | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Subscription Services** (monthly, quarterly, annual) | Within 7 days of payment date | Refundable after first payment |
| **Marketplace Products (Digital)** | Within 7 days before download/delivery | Not possible after delivery |
| **Marketplace Products (Physical)** | Within 7 days of product receipt | Return shipping cost borne by customer |
| **Tips (Gifts)** | Not possible immediately upon transaction | Excluded from withdrawal scope as one-time transaction |
| **Cafe Entry Ticket·Special Features** | Within 7 days before use | Not possible after use |

#### 4-2. Cases Where Withdrawal Is Not Possible (E-Commerce Act Article 17 Proviso)

Withdrawal is not possible in the following cases:

1. **Service Already Used or Consumed**
   - Service utilized during subscription period
   - Chat room access, forum browsing, voice chat participation
   - Downloaded digital content

2. **Value Diminishment Over Time**
   - After 7 days from subscription start: Not possible
   - Expiration of specific events/promotions

3. **Member's Liability**
   - Damage due to willful misconduct or gross negligence
   - Failure to follow usage instructions
   - Misuse or violation of terms

4. **Special Nature of Pi Transactions** ⚠️
   - **Blockchain Confirmation**: Once a Pi transaction is recorded on the blockchain, reversal is technically impossible
   - **Transaction Irreversibility**: Funds are immediately transferred from sender to recipient with no mid-transaction hold capability
   - **Company's Intermediary Role**: The Company only facilitates transactions; refund is not possible without final agreement between Members

## Part 4: Subscription Refunds

### Article 8: Subscription Refund Rules

#### 8-1. Monthly Subscription Refund
- **Within 7 days of payment date**: Full refund
- **After 7 days**: Pro rata refund for remaining period (remaining days ÷ 30 days × monthly payment)
- **Example**: 100 Pi monthly subscription, withdrawal request on day 10
  - Usage period: 10 days
  - Remaining period: 20 days
  - Refund amount: 20 ÷ 30 × 100 = approximately 67 Pi

#### 8-2. Quarterly·Annual Subscription Refund
- **Within 7 days of first payment**: Full refund
- **Days 7-30**: 75% refund (25% initial penalty)
- **30 days or later**: Pro rata refund by month
- **Automatic Renewal Block**: Automatic renewal stops after cancellation request

#### 8-3. Non-Refundable Items
- Costs for subscription periods already used
- Special promotional discount amounts (non-refundable)
- Duplicate payment errors: Refundable but only one correction per account

### Article 9: Refund of Auto-Renewal Subscriptions

#### 9-1. Auto-Renewal Subscription Cancellation
Members may cancel subscription at any time:
1. My Page > Subscription Management > "Cancel Subscription"
2. Payment stops upon cancellation request **at least 3 days before** next renewal date
3. Example: Renewal scheduled for June 10 → Must cancel by midnight June 7

#### 9-2. Refund Timeframe
- Refund processed within **3-5 business days** of cancellation request

## Part 5: Special Nature of Pi Virtual Asset and Disclaimer

### Article 10: Pi Virtual Asset Volatility Disclaimer ⚠️

#### 10-1. Irreversibility of Transaction
**Pi is a blockchain-based virtual asset with the following characteristics:**

1. **No Cancellation After Transaction Confirmation**
   - Transactions recorded on Pi blockchain are technically irreversible
   - Even central administrators (Company) cannot reverse transactions

2. **Peer-to-Peer Transfer Between Members**
   - Immediate transfer from sender's wallet to recipient's wallet
   - Does not pass through Company intermediary account
   - Recipient cannot unilaterally cancel after receiving funds

3. **Network Delays**
   - Possible transaction failures due to Pi Network blockchain delays or reorganization
   - Automatic recovery not guaranteed; Company provides mediation role

#### 10-2. Emergency Disclaimer for Price Fluctuations
Members may not claim damages from Company due to Pi value fluctuations at time of payment:
- Example: Purchase product for 100 Pi → After transaction confirmation Pi price drops 50% → Company bears no responsibility
- Virtual asset volatility is a risk Members must assume

#### 10-3. Company's Limitation of Liability
- Company is only a Pi payment intermediary with no warranty of virtual asset value
- Limited liability in case of blockchain network failures
- No warranty regarding legality or validity of Member-to-member transactions

### Article 15: Refund for Marketplace Seller Payment

#### 15-1. Refund to Sellers
- Due to buyer refunds, sellers must return received Pi
- If seller refuses return, buyer may file dispute mediation

#### 15-2. Seller Refund Refusal
- Company reviews transaction history if dispute occurs
- If legitimate refund reason confirmed, Company may order seller to process refund
- Non-compliance may result in seller account sanctions

### Article 16: Voice Chat Subscription Refund

#### 16-1. Voice Chat Subscription
- One-to-many voice chat channel access rights
- Monthly, quarterly, annual subscription available

#### 16-2. Refund Standards
- **Unused Status**: No chat room access after subscription start → Full refund within 7 days
- **Used Status**: After chat room access → Refund not possible

### Article 16-2: Bean Point Recharge Refund *(New in v1.1)*

#### 16-2-1. Eligible for Refund
- Only the **unused balance of Bean points directly recharged with Pi** by the Member.
- Bean granted free of charge through events, rewards, or promotions is not eligible for refund.

#### 16-2-2. Refund Standards
| Status | Refund |
|---|---|
| Within 7 days of recharge + **entirely unused** | Full recharged Pi refunded via A2U |
| Within 7 days of recharge + **partially used** | Unused balance converted at 100 Bean = 1 Pi and refunded via A2U (fractions below 1 Pi are truncated) |
| More than 7 days after recharge | No refund (usable within the Service only) |

#### 16-2-3. Notes
- Where free-granted and recharged Bean are mixed, usage is deemed to **deduct free-granted Bean first** when calculating the unused recharged balance.
- Remaining Bean expires upon membership withdrawal; a refund under the above standards may be requested before withdrawal.

### Article 16-3: Automatic Refund of Direct Pi Payments (A2U) *(New in v1.1)*

#### 16-3-1. Scope
Direct Pi payments to the Company such as cafe creation and subscriptions, and marketplace order payments.

#### 16-3-2. Processing
1. **Marketplace order cancellation**: Upon confirmed cancellation, the system executes an **automatic A2U refund** (cancellation fee deducted per Article 18).
2. **Other direct Pi payments**: Where withdrawal requirements (Article 4) are met, Pi is transferred from the Company wallet to the Member's Pi wallet via A2U (within 3-5 days).
3. A2U refunds are sent only to the **same Pi wallet** used for payment and may be delayed depending on blockchain network conditions.

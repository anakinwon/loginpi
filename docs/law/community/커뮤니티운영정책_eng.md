## Chapter 1. General Provisions

### Article 1 (Purpose)
This policy sets out the management standards and sanction procedures for all user-generated content (UGC) — including posts, chats, voice chats, and marketplace transactions — in order to maintain a healthy community environment within Cafe.pi (the "Service").

### Article 2 (Scope)
This policy applies to all of the following platform spaces:
1. **Boards**: notices, free posts, guides, marketplace boards
2. **Chat**: 1:1 personal chat, 1:N café chat rooms
3. **Voice chat**: 1:N voice chat, 1:1 calls
4. **Marketplace**: product descriptions, product reviews
5. **Profile**: nickname, bio, profile image

- **8-1. Company recording right**: when negative voice chat (abuse, threats, exposure of personal info) is reported, audio may be stored **for evidence**, used only for dispute resolution and police reporting.
- **8-2. Member recording prohibited**: members may not record others' voice chat without consent and post it. Violation: removal + 7-day suspension + police report.
- **8-3. Reporter protection**: a reporter's identity is kept confidential; retaliation against a reporter results in permanent ban + police report.

## Chapter 5. Sanctions Framework

### Article 11 (Sanction Tiers and Accumulation)
| Level | Description | Duration | Accumulation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Warning** (1 pt) | first minor violation, member notified | 3 within 2 months → Tier 1 | point-based |
| **Tier 1: Suspension** (3 pts) | posting/chat limited | 1–7 days | accumulate within 2 months → higher tier |
| **Tier 2: Heavy suspension** (5 pts) | all features limited (profile only) | 7–30 days | accumulate within 3 months → higher tier |
| **Tier 3: Permanent ban** | account deletion, no re-registration | indefinite | irreversible |

Accumulation rules: 3 warnings within 2 months → Tier 1; 2 Tier-1 within 3 months → Tier 2; 1 Tier-2 within 6 months → Tier 3. Severe violations (Article 3): immediate Tier 3. Suspensions are auto-restored on expiry; permanent bans may be appealed (Article 12).

### Article 12 (Notice and Appeals)
- **Notice**: by email, in-service notification, and My Page — stating the reason, basis, duration, and appeal method.
- **Appeal**: within **7 days** of notice, via My Page > Sanctions > "Appeal" or email (anakin.won@gmail.com), submitting the grounds and evidence. The Company re-reviews within 5 days and notifies the result by email.
- **Rejection**: if an appeal is rejected, no further appeal is possible; the member may pursue legal remedies (civil suit, consumer-agency mediation).

## Chapter 6. Copyright and IP Protection

### Article 13 (Copyright-Infringement Reporting — DMCA & Korean Copyright Act)
A rights holder or agent may report with: (1) rights-holder info, (2) original-work info (title, creation date, source), (3) infringing content (links, screenshots), (4) description of the infringement, (5) signature and contact. **Report email**: anakin.won@gmail.com.

Company process: confirm within 5 days → clear infringement removed immediately → ambiguous cases (quotation, parody) further reviewed → poster given 3 days to remove/edit → forced removal + warning/suspension on non-compliance. False reports lead to sanctions and possible damages claims.

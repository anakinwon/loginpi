# Consent to Collection and Use of Personal Information

> **[App Name]** (the "Company" or the "Service")
> Effective Date: ____ __, 20__

In accordance with Articles 15 and 22 of the Personal Information Protection Act of the Republic of Korea, the Company seeks your consent to collect and use your personal information as described below. Please read this notice carefully before deciding whether to consent.

---

## 1. Purposes of Collection and Use

1. Member identification, verification of intent to register, and identity verification
2. Provision and operation of the Service (chat, real-time translation, video calls, etc.)
3. Processing of paid services and management of payment records (including Pi payment processing and refunds)
4. Responding to customer inquiries and delivering notices
5. Prevention of fraudulent or unauthorized use and maintenance of service stability
6. Statistical analysis for service improvement (processed in a form that cannot identify individuals)

## 2. Personal Information Collected

### A. Required Items

| When Collected | Items |
|----------------|-------|
| Upon registration | Pi Network user identifier (UID), Pi username |
| During service use | Chat messages and conversation history, access date/time, IP address, device information (OS, browser type), service usage records |
| Upon paid transactions | Pi Payment ID, payment date/time, payment amount, transaction details (blockchain transaction ID) |

### B. Optional Items

| When Collected | Items | Purpose |
|----------------|-------|---------|
| Upon Kakao account linking | Kakao member ID, nickname, profile image, email | Social account linking and friend invitation features |
| Upon profile setup | Nickname, profile image, status message | Profile display and user identification |
| Upon using video calls | Video/audio streams (transmitted in real time only; not stored) | Provision of video call service |
| Upon using translation | Original text of messages submitted for translation | Provision of real-time translation service |

> ※ You may use the Service and its basic features without consenting to the optional items. However, the corresponding features (Kakao linking, video calls, etc.) may be unavailable.

### C. Automatically Collected Items

The following information may be automatically generated and collected during your use of the Service:

- Cookies, session information, access logs, service usage records, device identifiers

## 3. Retention and Use Period

The Company will **destroy personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been fulfilled**, except as follows:

### A. Retention Under Internal Company Policy

| Item | Retention Period | Reason |
|------|------------------|--------|
| Records of fraudulent use | 1 year after account deletion | Prevention of fraudulent registration and use |

### B. Retention Required by Applicable Laws

| Item | Retention Period | Governing Law |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| Records on contracts or withdrawal of offers | 5 years | Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (Korea) |
| Records on payments and supply of goods/services | 5 years | Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (Korea) |
| Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution | 3 years | Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (Korea) |
| Access records (login logs, etc.) | 3 months | Protection of Communications Secrets Act (Korea) |

> ※ Due to the technical nature of blockchain, transaction records stored on the Pi Network blockchain may be impossible to delete. The Company does not record directly identifiable personal information on the blockchain.

## 5. Right to Refuse Consent and Consequences of Refusal

You have the right to refuse consent to the collection and use of your personal information.

- Refusal of **required items**: Registration and use of the Service will not be possible.
- Refusal of **optional items**: Only the corresponding features will be restricted; basic services remain available.

## 6. Rights of Data Subjects

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

1. Request access to your personal information
2. Request correction of errors
3. Request deletion
4. Request suspension of processing
5. Withdraw consent (via [Settings > Delete Account] in the app or through customer support)

Rights may be exercised through the in-app settings menu or the contact information below, and the Company will take action without delay.

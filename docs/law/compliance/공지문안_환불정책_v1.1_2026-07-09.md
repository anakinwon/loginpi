## English (for posting)

**Title**: [Notice] Refund and Withdrawal Policy Revision (Effective July 16, 2026)

Hello, this is Cafe.pi, operator of PyCafé™ and PyShop™.

Our Refund and Withdrawal Policy has been revised as follows to reflect expanded payment options.

**Key Changes (v1.0 → v1.1)**

1. **New Bean point recharge refund standards** — The unused balance of Bean points recharged with Pi may be refunded in Pi (100 Bean = 1 Pi) within 7 days of recharge. Bean granted free through events or rewards is not refundable; usage is deemed to deduct free grants first.
2. **Automatic refund for direct Pi payments** — Upon marketplace order cancellation, the system automatically refunds to the Member's Pi wallet (A2U); other direct Pi payments meeting withdrawal requirements are refunded the same way within 3-5 days, only to the wallet used for payment.
3. **Marketplace cancellation fees specified** — For Member-attributable cancellations (e.g., change of mind), fees of 0.9 Pi (buyer) / 1.1 Pi (seller) are deducted, disclosed in advance on the cancellation screen. No fee when the Company or counterparty is at fault.
4. **Clerical corrections** — Return shipping cost notation (currency unit error) corrected to actual courier rates, among other text fixes.

**Effective Date**: July 16, 2026
**Full text**: App footer → Refund and Withdrawal Policy

Transactions before the effective date remain governed by the previous policy. Contact: anakin.won@gmail.com

Thank you.
